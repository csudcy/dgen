Saving to PDF

To save the cards as a PDF (so you can e.g. email them to someone), you can:
  1. Make sure you are using the Chrome browser*!
  2. Click the print button below
  3. The print dialog should popup
  4. Check the "Destination" on the left says "Save as PDF"
    • If not, click "Change" and select "ave as PDF"
  5. Click "Save"
  6. Choose where you want to save your PDF & what to call it
  7. Click "Save" again
  8. You should now have a PDF saved - enjoy playing your new game!
*This may work in other browsers but I haven't tested them - good luck!
